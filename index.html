About

I am a tech enthusiast with expertise in various programming languages such as Android, Java, Python, Firebase, Laravel, React Native, C++, C, and Flutter Basic. Additionally, I have experience in using Git for version control. I am a quick learner and adaptive in nature, which allows me to stay up to date with the latest advancements in the field. My excellent coordination and decision-making skills, along with my leadership abilities, have made me a valuable asset to various teams I've worked with.

Apart from coding, my interests and hobbies include writing, travelling, and singing. I am fluent in Hindi, English, and Maithili.

Achievements, Certifications & Awards:
I am proud to have received several awards and certifications throughout my academic and professional journey. Recently, I was the 1st Runner Up at the Rajasthan IT Day <HACK/ATHON> with team 101 Found, and we won a 20 lakh work order from the Government of Rajasthan. Our android application tackled the problem of Financial Inclusion and Economic Empowerment. In addition, I was also the 1st Runner Up at the Game of Code'22, an 8-day coding boot camp organized by Chandigarh University. I have completed a Software Testing Certificate from NPTEL and a Crash Course on Python from Coursera.

Apart from my technical skills, I am also a winner of the Tech. Quiz competition in HEA-Polytechnic during my diploma and have won multiple competitions and sports during my school days. Overall, my diverse experiences, skills, and achievements demonstrate my ability to take on complex challenges and deliver high-quality solutions.
